Chicken and Veggie Stirfry

Description

A chicken and veggie stirfry is a quick and easy yet nutrional dinner to have when you don't have the time to cook something extravagent but still desire the taste of something great

Ingrediants

  • 1 lb chicken
  • Salt and peppers
  • 1 lb broccoli
  • 8 oz mushroom
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 3 clives garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup flour

Steps

  1. n a large pan on medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of oil.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add chicken, season with salt and pepper, and sauté until cooked through and browned
  3. Remove cooked chicken from pan and set aside
  4. In the same pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and add mushrooms
  5. When the mushrooms start to soften, add broccoli florets and stir-fry until the broccoli is tender
  6. Remove cooked mushrooms and broccoli from the pan and set aside
  7. Add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant
  8. Add the remaining sauce ingredients and stir until smooth
  9. Return the chicken and vegetables to the saucy pan, stir until heated through
  10. Serve with hot rice or noodles
